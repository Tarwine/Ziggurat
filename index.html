Born from an Orlando 48-hour Gamejam, Ziggurat is a first-person, puzzle platformer built for the Oculus Rift VR experience. We have a desktop version avaliable for play and a downloadable VR version.


Enjoy the game? If you did, let us know! We love the feedback! And with enough support, we will take this quick, sloppy contest entry and make it a full-fledged, professional polished game.

But only if you guys are down, you down?


We also have an Oculus Rift compatible version, for free!
