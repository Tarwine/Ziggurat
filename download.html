**Requires the Oculus Rift**

 

 

Controls:

Keyboard

WASD - Movement

Mouse - Horizontal camera

Space - Jump

E (hold) - Swing on rope

Right Ctrl - Reset Oculus head tracking

 

 

360 Controller

Left Analog Stick - Movement

Right Analog Stick - Horizontal camera

A - Jump

Right Trigger (hold)- Swing on rope

Back - Reset Oculus head tracking
